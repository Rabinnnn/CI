About Community Mentorship and Empowerment Initiative

ABOUT US

Community Mentorship and Empowerment (COME) Initiative is a Youth Led-Community Based Organization that Creates a Supportive Environment FOR Youth, Adolescent Girls and Young Women to Build Sustainable, Peaceful, Tolerant and Inclusive Community through Life Skills, Talent and ICT Development.

Our Mission

To Impart Value Based Skills to the Young People by Building their Capacity and that of their Influencers for Socio-Economic Development.

Our Vision

To Build a Community that is able to Make Responsible and Informed Choices in Life.

Core Values

Committed to building an Empowered communit.y

Inclusivity for all regardless of ability, tribe or creed.

Integrity and strong morality in our programs.
