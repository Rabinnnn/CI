Sauti Kimya

Sauti Kimya focuses on capacity building training on use of Participatory Theatre, Serial Radio Drama, social media (Facebook twitter, and other tools) for Sexual Reproductive Health Education and awareness, participatory M&E etc. Engaging young people as social media content developers and content creators early on builds positive anticipation and support for the resulting products and programmes, as well as fostering Sense of ownership because users ‘opinions will have helped to guide their development and implementation..

Life Skills Training at Muhoroni Youth Empowerement Centre (Kisumu County)

Life Skills training is a program model and resource package that seeks to support, protect, and empower adolescent girls in humanitarian settings. Girl Shine has been designed to help contribute to the improved prevention and response to violence against adolescent girls in humanitarian settings by providing them with the skills and knowledge to identify types of GBV and seek support services if they experience or are at risk of Gender Based Violence (GBV).

Digital Skills Training at Muhoroni Youth Empowerment Centre (Kisumu County))

The digital skills training exposes participants to various online marketing concepts such as search engine marketing so that they can optimize their business as well as do the same for other businesses at a fee. At the end of the training, the participants will have not only a certificate but most importantly skills on how to build an online presence and further create content. With an expected half a billion internet users in Africa by the end of 2020, there are huge opportunities for African businesses and young digital entrepreneurs

About Us

Community Mentorship and Empowerment Initiative is a youth led-community centered NGO dedicated to promoting innovative ways of working with rapidly growing media and technology to tackle communications challenges presented by conflict, culture, fragile governance, poverty and environment.
